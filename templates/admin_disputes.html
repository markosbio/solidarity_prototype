⚖️ Disputes & Payment Holds

Review member-filed disputes on provider payments. Apply holds, resolve disputes, or reverse fraudulent payments.

How members file a dispute Members can dispute any provider payment by visiting /dispute/<ref> (linked from their transaction history). Once filed, the payment appears here with status open. You have four tools below to act on it.

🔒 Place Hold

Freezes the payment so the provider cannot withdraw it. Use this while you investigate. Does not refund anything yet.

✅ Resolve

Closes the dispute in the member's favour or confirms the payment was legitimate. Marks the dispute resolved — no money moves.

🚫 Dismiss

Rejects the member's dispute claim. Payment stays with the provider. Use when the dispute is unfounded.

↩️ Reverse Payment

Returns the payment amount to the community pool. The provider no longer receives the funds. This is permanent and logged in the audit trail. Only Super Admins can reverse.
