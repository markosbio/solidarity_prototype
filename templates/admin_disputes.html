📋 Care Quality Reports

Members can report issues with a provider — treatment not given, overcharging, or poor care. Review reports and take action below.

How members file a report Members visit /report-care/<ref> from their transaction history and choose a category (not treated, overcharged, poor care, etc.). Reports appear here with status open. Because payments go directly to providers' mobile wallets, admin cannot reverse a mobile money transfer. Instead, you can restore the community pool balance from platform reserves as compensation, and suspend the provider to protect future members.
